Complete Artwork from Ludvig - finished in 2024

This artwork was done in 2024 and was created using a thick A3 paper, alcohol-baser markers, fineliners, ink pens and glue pens/markers. The paper came from a collection from "Akademibokhandeln". The alcohol based markers were from Copic which is an Japanese brand that make professional alhohol markers with a one side of the pen being a brush which is superduper awesome when you do layers and mix colors. I Also used different fineliners and epsecially different glue pens which you can see outlining many of the figures.
